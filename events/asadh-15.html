Asadh 15

Celebrating National Paddy Day with plantation activities and cultural programs.

Asadh 15 marks the beginning of paddy plantation season in Nepal. Our school celebrates this important agricultural day with tree plantation drives, educational activities about farming, and cultural performances that honor our agricultural heritage.
