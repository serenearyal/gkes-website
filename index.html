Dear Guardians and Students,

Dear Guardians and Students,

It gives me great pleasure to share a few words about our vision at Gyankunja English School (GKES). Our mission is to empower students and help them achieve their full potential. Education today goes beyond acquiring knowledge - it includes building character, developing skills, and preparing students for a successful future.

In this rapidly changing global world, exposure to diverse learning experiences is essential. We strive to nurture discipline, compassion, respect for culture, social responsibility, and leadership qualities in our students, helping them grow into confident and capable individuals.

At GKES, we are committed to creating a supportive environment of mutual respect, cooperation, and excellence. With the combined efforts of students, guardians, and teachers, we are confident that our learners will achieve all-round development and contribute positively to society and the nation.

Hiralal Aryal

Principal, Gyankunja English School
